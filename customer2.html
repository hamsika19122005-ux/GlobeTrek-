Cultural Journey Tour (5 Days, 4 Nights)

📍 Ancient Cultural Triangle (Sigiriya / Kandy)

🗿 Explore ancient rock fortresses, sacred temple shrines, and traditional heritage trails

About this Tour

Immerse yourself in the rich history and deep heritage of Sri Lanka's cultural heartland. This premium, pre-vetted 5-day journey takes you deep into the heart of ancient kingdoms. Wander through centuries-old ruins, marvel at structural wonders like the Sigiriya Rock Citadel, and witness beautiful traditional dance performances. Curated specially for history enthusiasts, photographers, and travelers seeking an authentic spiritual exploration.

Inclusion Notes: Daily traditional buffet breakfasts, certified English-speaking heritage guides, all archaeological site entry passes, and air-conditioned luxury transport transfers are fully included.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Historic Polonnaruwa Ancient Ruins Exploration +
Day 2: Morning Sigiriya Citadel Climb & Traditional Village Lunch +
Day 3: Dambulla Cave Complex Tour & Journey to Hill Country Capital (Kandy) +
Day 4: Sacred Temple of the Tooth Relic & Royal Botanical Gardens Walk +
Day 5: Traditional Kandyan Dance Showcase, Spice Garden Tour & Departure +
