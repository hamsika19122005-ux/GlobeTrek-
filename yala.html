Yala Wildlife Safari

3 Days 4.9 (112 Reviews)

Witness majestic elephants, leopards, and rare birds in their natural habitats. Yala National Park offers one of the highest leopard densities in the world, making this wild journey an unforgettable adventure for wildlife photographers and nature lovers alike.

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Evening Golden Hour Drive

Arrive at your luxury campsite or jungle resort near the park borders. Embark on an introductory late-afternoon safari drive as animals gather near waterholes during sunset.

Day 2: Full-Day Deep Jungle Exploration

Set out before dawn with packed meals. Track the elusive Sri Lankan leopard, look out for sloth bears, massive herds of elephants, and cross deep into the park's untouched coastal wetlands.

Day 3: Sunrise Bird Watching & Departure

Enjoy an early morning nature walk along the buffer zones to spot endemic and migratory tropical birds before concluding your wild expedition.
