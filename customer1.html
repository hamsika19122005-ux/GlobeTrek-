Coastal Escape Tour (3 Days, 2 Nights)

📍 Western Coast (Negombo)

🌊 Sun-kissed golden shores, historic Dutch canals, and vibrant lagoon life

About this Tour

Relax on tropical golden sands, tour Dutch colonial canals, and sample fresh seafood markets. This pre-vetted trip takes you through the historical fishing town of Negombo, offering the perfect blend of relaxation and cultural exploration along Sri Lanka's beautiful Western coastline. Perfect for couples, families, and seafood enthusiasts alike.

Inclusion Notes: All breakfasts, certified boat tour guides, historical site entry fees, and local canal transport tickets are fully included and pre-vetted.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival, Negombo Lagoon & Dutch Canal Boat Tour +

Arrive and check into your coasral resort. In the afternoon, embark on a relaxing boat ride through the historic Hamilton Dutch Canal and Negombo Lagoon. Observe local fisherman casting traditional nets and catch a stunning sunset over the mangrove waters.

Day 2: Morning Seafood Market Exploration & Golden Beach Relaxation +

Experience the bustling energy of the famous Negombo Lellama seafood market at sunrise. Spend the afternoon unwinding on the wide golden sandy beaches, or swimming in the warm waters of the Indian Ocean before enjoying a fresh beachside seafood dinner barbecue.

Day 3: Historical Churches Cycling Tour & Departure +

Take a guided bicycle tour through the city streets to admire the architecture of centuries-old historic churches. Shop for local spices, handicrafts, and souvenirs along the lively beach roads before arranging your final check-out and transit depature to the nearby international airport.
