Ancient Heritage Trail

5 Days 4.9 (124 Reviews)

Journey deep into the Cultural Triangle of Sri Lanka. Explore ancient ruins, majestic rock fortresses, and sacred kingdoms that date back over 2,500 years. This comprehensive historical experience brings old-world stories to life with expert local guides.

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Travel to Anuradhapura

Welcome to Sri Lanka! Travel in comfort to the first ancient capital city, check into your hotel, and enjoy a traditional dinner welcoming ceremony.

Day 2: The Sacred Sacred City of Anuradhapura

Explore ruins of huge brick stupas, ancient monasteries, and the sacred Jaya Sri Maha Bodhi tree—the oldest historically documented tree in the world.

Day 3: The Medieval Capital of Polonnaruwa

Cycle through the incredibly preserved stone statues of Gal Vihara and the ancient Royal Palace ruins of King Parakramabahu.

Day 4: The Sigiriya Rock Fortress

Climb the breathtaking 1,200 steps of the 8th Wonder of the World. Marvel at the ancient frescoes, the Mirror Wall, and the massive Lion Paws at the summit gateway.

Day 5: Dambulla Cave Temple & Departure

Tour the beautifully preserved cave monastery system before heading back to the airport or your next destination.
