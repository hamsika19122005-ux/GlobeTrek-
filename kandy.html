Cultural Kandy Escapade

2 Days 4.7 (84 Reviews)

Visit the sacred Temple of the Tooth Relic and stroll around Kandy Lake. Immerse yourself in the last royal capital of Sri Lanka, surrounded by scenic mist-covered hills, rich heritage, vibrant traditional arts, and lush botanical gardens.

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Royal Botanical Gardens & Traditional Dance Show

Arrive in Kandy and visit the expansive Royal Botanical Gardens in Peradeniya, famous for its grand orchid collection. In the evening, witness a spectacular traditional Kandyan cultural dance and fire-walking performance.

Day 2: Sacred Tooth Relic Temple & Kandy Lake Walk

Experience the morning 'Thewava' ritual at the sacred Temple of the Tooth Relic (Sri Dalada Maligawa). Conclude your getaway with a scenic, peaceful walk around the historic Kandy Lake before heading home.
