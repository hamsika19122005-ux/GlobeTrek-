Misty Hills & Tea Estates

7 Days 5.0 (156 Reviews)

Journey through the scenic waterfalls and lush tea plantations of Ella and Nuwara Eliya. Breathe in the crisp mountain air, trek through emerald-green hills, and witness architectural marvels nestled deep within the misty highlands.

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Little England (Nuwara Eliya)

Travel up into the cool highlands. Check into your colonial-style resort, stroll along the banks of Lake Gregory, and enjoy the crisp mountain climate.

Day 2: Tea Factory Plantation & Processing Tour

Walk through endless rows of emerald tea bushes. Visit a working processing factory to see how Ceylon tea is made, followed by a private tea-tasting masterclass.

Day 3: Scenic Highland Train Ride to Ella

Board the famous mountain train crossing old stone viaducts. Experience one of the world's most beautiful rail journeys through mist, pine forests, and cascading waterfalls.

Day 4: Trekking Little Adam's Peak

Take an early morning hike up Little Adam’s Peak for panoramic views across the Ella Gap, followed by an exhilarating zipline ride over the tree canopies.

Day 5: The Iconic Nine Arch Bridge

Stand on the historic colonial-era railway bridge hidden in the jungle. Watch the local trains pass overhead and capture incredible structural landscape photos.

Day 6: Diyaluma Waterfall Exploration

Hike to the upper natural pools of Sri Lanka's second-highest waterfall. Swim in crystal-clear mountain water right on the cliff's edge.

Day 7: Ravana Caves & Return Journey

Explore the legendary Ravana cave complex linked to ancient epics before descending the hills to complete your wilderness expedition.
