Adventure Hike Expedition

3 Days 4.9 (92 Reviews)

Trek through lush green tea estates, climb the rugged cliffs of Ella Rock, and stand over the architectural marvel of the Nine Arch Bridge. This thrill-seeker's getaway pairs high-altitude mountain trekking with the most dramatic landscape panoramas in the central highlands.

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Scenic Highland Train Ride & Nine Arch Trek

Arrive via one of the world's most beautiful train routes into Ella. In the afternoon, take a light pine-forest trek down to the iconic Nine Arch Bridge just in time to watch the evening train pass over the majestic brick arches.

Day 2: Sunrise Ella Rock Summit Challenge

Set out before dawn along local railway tracks and rushing single tracks. Push through dense cloud forests and steep rock faces to reach the summit of Ella Rock for a breathtaking, unobstructed view across the mountain gap.

Day 3: Diyaluma Falls Abseiling & Departure

Conclude your expedition with a short trip to Sri Lanka's second-highest waterfall. Enjoy a refreshing dip in the natural upper pools and optionally try controlled cliff abseiling before your return journey.
