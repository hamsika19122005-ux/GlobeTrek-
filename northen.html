Discover the Jaffna Peninsula, the vibrant Nallur Kovil, and serene northern islands. Immerse yourself in a region filled with distinct traditions, majestic golden architecture, rich historic forts, and unforgettable culinary specialties.

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Journey to Jaffna & Sunset at Casuarina Beach

Travel north past the scenic Elephant Pass to arrive in Jaffna city. Check into your hotel and spend a relaxing evening walking along the soft white sands of Casuarina Beach.

Day 2: Nallur Kandaswamy Kovil & Jaffna Public Library

Experience the grand golden architecture and peaceful rituals of the historic Nallur Kandaswamy Kovil. Later, tour the majestic Jaffna Public Library and explore the massive star-shaped coastal Jaffna Fort built by the Dutch.

Day 3: Island Ferry Adventure to Nainativu

Take a scenic morning boat ferry across the Palk Strait to Nainativu Island. Visit the sacred Nagapooshani Amman Kovil and the historical Nagadeepa Purana Viharaya temple complex.

Day 4: Keerimalai Sacred Springs & Departure

Visit the ancient Keerimalai hot springs sitting right next to the ocean. Taste authentic local Jaffna snacks like custom palmyrah products before starting your return journey.
