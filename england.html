Little England Getaway

2 Days 4.6 (64 Reviews)

Enjoy the cool breeze, colonial architecture, and beautiful Gregory Lake park views. Escape up into the serene mountain climate of Nuwara Eliya, affectionately known as Little England, where old-world British charm meets pristine green highlands.

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Colonial City Tour & Gregory Lake Relaxing

Arrive in the refreshing mountain air of Nuwara Eliya. Take a guided architectural walk past the historic red-brick Post Office and iconic colonial bungalows. Spend your evening relaxing with a boat ride on the peaceful waters of Lake Gregory.

Day 2: Victoria Park Stroll & Tea Lounge Experience

Begin your morning with a quiet walk among the manicured flower beds and towering trees of Victoria Park. Conclude your high-altitude escape with an authentic English afternoon tea service at a heritage hill-country property before descending back down.
