Galle Dutch Fort Voyage

3 Days 4.9 (118 Reviews)

Walk down historical cobblestone streets and view the iconic ocean lighthouse. Step into a perfectly preserved living UNESCO World Heritage site where centuries-old European architecture meets the vibrant, crashing waves of the southern Indian Ocean.

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Sunset Rampart Walk

Arrive in the historic city of Galle. Check into your boutique hotel within the fort walls and spend your evening walking the massive stone ramparts as the sun sets over the ocean.

Day 2: Historic Colonial Exploration & Lighthouse Tour

Take a guided morning walk past colonial Dutch villas, churches, and museums. Visit the famous white Galle Lighthouse and explore the vibrant local artisan boutiques, gelaterias, and cafes lining the cobblestone paths.

Day 3: Unawatuna Beach & Stilt Fishermen Experience

Head to nearby Unawatuna beach for a relaxing morning swim. Photograph the iconic traditional Sri Lankan stilt fishermen in action before wrapping up your coastal voyage.
