Relax and unwind along the coastal shores of Negombo and Mirissa. Escape the hustle of daily life on pristine golden sands, enjoy breathtaking tropical sunsets, and experience world-renowned ocean activities like whale watching and coastal boat tours.

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Coastal Arrival & Negombo Lagoon Tour

Arrive along the sunny coast of Negombo. Check into your beachfront resort and take a peaceful evening boat ride down the historic Dutch canals and local lagoons.

Day 2: Travel to Mirissa & Sunset Beach Walk

Journey south to the vibrant beach town of Mirissa. Spend your afternoon sunbathing, swimming in crystal-clear turquoise waters, and catching a legendary ocean sunset at Coconut Tree Hill.

Day 3: Whale Watching Adventure & Departure

Embark on an early morning deep-sea cruise to spot majestic Blue Whales and playful dolphins in their natural habitat before wrapping up your tropical escape.
